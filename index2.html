The NPCs

AI contolled tanks that may or may not try to kill you.

Bot

Not the smartest of tanks, but can pack a punch. Bots on your team won't attack you, use this to your advantage!

Dominator

Stationary tank that can be captured and be made to fight alongside your team. Be wary though, enemies can capture your Dominator at any time!

Base

Invincible tank that can be found at spawn points/bases. Makes quick work of any enemy tanks willing to lock eyes with it.

Crasher

Barely a threat on their own, but large numbers will pose a great challenge.

Sentry

Occasionally found in place of a crasher, they will vary in power based on what weapon they're carrying.

Boss

Originally quite weak, they've been buffed in this iteration of arras.io. Oh, did I mention they're a lot more "intimidating"?
